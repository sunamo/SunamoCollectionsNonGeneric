# NuGet Alternatives to SunamoCollectionsNonGeneric

This document lists popular NuGet packages that provide similar functionality to SunamoCollectionsNonGeneric.

## Overview

Non-generic collection utilities

## Alternative Packages

### System.Collections
- **NuGet**: System.Collections.NonGeneric
- **Purpose**: Built-in non-generic collections
- **Key Features**: ArrayList, Hashtable, Queue, Stack

### System.Collections.Specialized
- **NuGet**: System.Collections.Specialized
- **Purpose**: Specialized collections
- **Key Features**: NameValueCollection, StringCollection, BitVector32

## Comparison Notes

Non-generic collections are legacy. Prefer generic collections (List<T>, Dictionary<K,V>) for new code.

## Choosing an Alternative

Consider these alternatives based on your specific needs:
- **System.Collections**: Built-in non-generic collections
- **System.Collections.Specialized**: Specialized collections
